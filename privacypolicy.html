Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/10/2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Google OAuth 2.0 service to interact with our application hosted on https://j2forem.github.io/.

Information We Collect

When you sign in using Google OAuth 2.0, we collect and store certain information from your Google account, including:

  • Basic profile information: Name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Google Sheets access: With your explicit permission, we may read from and write to Google Sheets based on your interactions with the app.

We do not collect any sensitive information such as passwords or credit card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected from Google OAuth 2.0 and Google Sheets API for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To allow you to securely sign in using your Google account.
  • Interacting with Google Sheets: To fetch and update data from Google Sheets as needed by the app.
  • Improving the service: We may use anonymous, non-personally identifiable data to monitor and improve the app’s performance and functionality.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following situations:

  • With your consent: If we explicitly request and you provide consent to share your information.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, regulation, or legal process, we may disclose information to comply with government or legal authorities.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

Your Choices

You have the option to:

  • Revoke access: You can revoke the app’s access to your Google account by visiting your Google Account Permissions and removing the app's access.
  • Control information: You can choose to provide only the minimum amount of information necessary to use our service.
